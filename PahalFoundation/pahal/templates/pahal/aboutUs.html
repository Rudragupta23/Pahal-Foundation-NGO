About Us

"Taking the First Step Towards a Brighter Future."

Our Story

At Pahal Foundation, we believe that every child deserves a chance to dream, learn, and grow, regardless of their background. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between privilege and need, Pahal Foundation is dedicated to supporting underprivileged children, especially those from the families of daily wage workers and marginalized communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerfulâ€”to ensure that no child is deprived of education, essential clothing, and the basic necessities that every child deserves. We aim to break the cycle of poverty through quality education, skill development, and holistic support.

Our Vision

We envision a society where children are not defined by their circumstances but by their dreams and potential. Our goal is to create an environment where education is a right, not a privilege, and where every child feels seen, heard, and valued.

Why We Exist

We exist because millions of children in India face barriers to education, nutrition, and basic healthcare. Without intervention, these children often fall into cycles of poverty, making it difficult for them to escape their circumstances. At Pahal Foundation, we strive to provide a pathway for these children to access education, build skills, and create a better future for themselves and their families.

What We Do

Our work involves providing free education, distributing essential resources like books, clothes, and food, and conducting skill-development programs for underprivileged children. We collaborate with volunteers and organizations to create safe learning environments and ensure long-term positive change in the lives of these children.
